ABOUT OUR DEVELOPMENT

Our system offers the creation of Jingles using a neural network that was trained using musical interval data. In addition to its main functionality, the site offers concepts, analysis and examples about this universe. In addition to a tutorial explaining the application. For development, we rely on the guidance of the teachers of the subjects for English for Specific Purposes and Human-Computer Interface.

For the development we used the programming languages: HTML, CSS, Ajax, JavaScript and Python. The design was designed to remember the musical universe. So both the color palette and the The arrangement of the elements influenced the construction of the interface. The construction of both the name and the were soon created from the blending (BLEND) of the words "music" and "Jingle" originating Mingle, which can also be interpreted as mix or mix.
